# Grant Awarded

## Flamma Network Grant Program Highlights

The Flamma Network Grant Program (FNGP) is dedicated to supporting innovative projects that contribute to the ecosystem's growth and advancement. Here are the highlighted programs that have recently received grants:

### 1. Testnet Faucet Grant Program (TFGP)

The Testnet Faucet Grant Program (TFGP) is designed to provide developers and users with easy access to test tokens on the Flamma Testnet. This initiative aims to foster a more robust testing environment, enabling more efficient development and testing of decentralized applications (DApps) and smart contracts.

- Grant Award: FLA 6.8 million

- Total Budget: $29,000

- Lock-Up Period: 3 years

**Program Overview:** TFGP addresses the need for accessible test tokens, which are crucial for developers to simulate real-world scenarios and ensure the reliability of their applications before deploying them on the mainnet. By offering a steady supply of test tokens, the program helps streamline the development process and reduces barriers for new developers entering the Flamma ecosystem.

**Impact:** The TFGP has significantly improved the developer experience on the Flamma Testnet. By providing ample test tokens, it has facilitated the creation and testing of numerous DApps, contributing to a more vibrant and active developer community.

### 3. FLA Wallet Grant Program (FWGP)

The FLA Wallet Grant Program (FWGP) is designed to develop a comprehensive and user-friendly digital wallet specifically for the Flamma Network. This wallet aims to provide users with secure, intuitive, and feature-rich functionalities for managing their FLA tokens and interacting with the Flamma ecosystem.

- Grant Award: FLA 30 million

- Total Budget: $100,000

- Lock-Up Period: 3 years

**Program Overview:** FWGP focuses on building the FLA Wallet, which will support core functionalities such as sending, receiving, and storing FLA tokens. Additionally, the wallet will include advanced features like staking, participating in governance, and seamless integration with decentralized applications (DApps) within the Flamma ecosystem. The FLA Wallet will also support cross-chain asset transfers, enabling users to bridge assets between Flamma and other blockchain networks.

**Impact:** The FLA Wallet will significantly enhance the user experience and functionality within the Flamma Network. By offering a dedicated and feature-rich wallet, the program will encourage greater participation and engagement in the ecosystem.

### 5. COCO Grant Program (CGP)

The COCO Grant Program (CGP) aims to address the need for user asset transactions to be handled by smart contracts on the Flamma Network. This initiative will support and solve the lack of an NFT marketplace in the early Flamma ecosystem, providing a decentralized platform for NFT transactions.

- Grant Award: FLA 13.24 million

- Total Budget: $40,000

- Lock-Up Period: 3 years

**Program Overview:** The COCO Grant Program (CGP) is designed to support the creation of COCO, a decentralized NFT marketplace on the Flamma Network. This platform will enable users to trade NFTs directly through smart contracts, ensuring a decentralized and secure trading environment.

**Impact:** COCO will significantly enhance the Flamma Network by providing a robust and user-friendly NFT marketplace. By facilitating decentralized transactions, COCO will attract a diverse user base, drive economic activity, and increase transaction volumes, contributing to the overall growth and adoption of the Flamma Network.
