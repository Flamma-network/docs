# Flamma SureAirdrop: 400 Million FLA Tokens for Participants

## SureAirdrop Distribution Rules

The 400 million FLA tokens will be distributed as follows:

1. **20% for Active Testnet Participants:**
- Allocation: 80 million FLA
- Eligibility: Users who actively participate in the Flamma Testnet, providing valuable feedback and contributing to its development.

2. **60% for Mainnet Bridge Participants:**
- Allocation: 240 million FLA
- Eligibility: Users who participate in bridging assets to the Flamma Mainnet, ensuring liquidity and cross-chain interoperability.

3. **10% for Ethereum/L2s Contributors:**
- Allocation: 40 million FLA
- Eligibility: GitHub contributors to [Ethereum](https://github.com/ethereum/go-ethereum) and L2s solutions e.g. Optimism (OP), Arbitrum (ARB), Base, Blast, zkPolygon. Contributions to these ecosystems demonstrate a commitment to advancing blockchain technology.

4. **10% for SureAirdrop by Ecosystem Partners:**
- Allocation: 40 million FLA
- Eligibility: Early projects and partners that have collaborated with the Flamma ecosystem, helping to build and expand the network.

## Participation and Rewards

Participants can earn **[points](/flapoints.mdx)** by completing various quests, which will then translate into FLA rewards. The system automatically updates rewards daily at UTC time, ensuring timely and accurate distribution of incentives.

### How to Participate

1. **Active Testnet Participants:**
- Earn points for each completed quest, contributing to your overall airdrop reward.

2. **Mainnet Bridge Participants:**
- Each successful bridge transaction earns points, which accumulate towards your airdrop allocation.

3. **Ethereum/L2s Contributors:**
- Contribute to the development of Ethereum (e.g. [go-ethereum](https://github.com/ethereum/go-ethereum)) and L2s solutions via GitHub.
- Verified contributions will be rewarded with points, contributing to your total FLA airdrop.

4. **SureAirdrop by Ecosystem Partners:**
- Ecosystem partner projects will gradually be listed on the rewards page.
- Participants can earn rewards by interacting with these ecosystem projects.

### Daily Rewards Update
- Points and corresponding FLA rewards are updated daily at 00:00Z.
- Participants can track their progress and rewards through the dashboard.
- Ensure you regularly complete quests and contribute to maintain and increase your points tally.

The Flamma SureAirdrop program is a significant opportunity for users and contributors to be rewarded for active participation and contributions to the ecosystem. Flamma aims to incentivize engagement, enhance network security, and foster a thriving community. Participate now to earn your share of FLA and help grow the Flamma Network.

### Referral code

Participants need to bridge at least 0.005 ETH to unlock a referral code and earn up to 5,000 points. Once unlocked, you can receive 15% of the points earned by friends who complete quests.

<FAQBox title="What is the difference between Airdrop and SureAirdrop?">
  - A crypto airdrop is a strategy used by blockchain startups to distribute tokens or coins to specific wallet addresses. Usually, this strategy is uncertain.
  - SureAirdrop, pioneered by Flamma, aims to accurately calculate mainnet token rewards based on participants' contributions to the Flamma network using an algorithm. Participants earn mapped points by completing quests. These points correspond to **Expected Rewards in FLA**, which will not be diluted or reduced.

</FAQBox>
