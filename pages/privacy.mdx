# Flamma Network Privacy Policy
UPDATED: JULY 2024

## Introduction

The Flamma Network values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to ensure your data is protected.

We appreciate your trust in us when you use our products and services, and when you visit our websites, including https://flamma.network/, https://app.flamma.network/, and https://metamask.io/ (collectively referred to as the "Sites"). This Privacy Notice describes how we handle your information on the Sites (including subdomains), applications, products, and services (collectively referred to as the "Services") where this notice is posted, linked, or referenced.

## Our Privacy Commitments

We have updated our Privacy Notice to ensure transparency regarding our data processing activities. For users of Flamma, this means that we:

- Do not collect your private keys.
- Do not sell your Personal Information.
- Do not collect or retain Personal Information unless it is necessary to provide you with our Services and enhance your user experience.
- Do not collect financial payment.

## Information Collection

- Personal Information: We may collect personal information such as your wallet address and transaction data to provide and improve our services.
- Usage Data: Information on how you interact with the Flamma Network, including the pages visited, time spent on the site, and other usage metrics.

## Use of Information

- Transaction Processing: To facilitate transactions and reward distribution within the Flamma Network.
- Service Improvement: To enhance the functionality and user experience of our platform.

## Data Protection

- Security Measures: We implement robust security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.
- Encryption: Data is encrypted during transmission and storage to ensure privacy and security.

## Disclosure of Information
- Third-Party Services: We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to outside parties. Information may be disclosed if required by law or to protect our rights.
- Compliance: We may share data with third-party services that help us operate our platform, subject to their own privacy policies.

## Cookies
- Usage: The Flamma Network uses cookies to enhance user experience and analyze website traffic. Users can control the use of cookies through their browser settings.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

- Updates: We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on our website, and users are encouraged to review the policy periodically.

## Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@flamma.network.
