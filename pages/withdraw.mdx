# Withdraw Assets

Flamma Network allows for easy asset withdrawal, providing users with the flexibility to move their funds freely. This tutorial will guide you through the process of withdrawing ETH, USDT, and USDC from Flamma Network to other supported networks (Ethereum, Optimism (OP), Arbitrum (ARB), Base). Note that there is a mandatory 3-day verification period for withdrawals.

## Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

Connect Your Wallet to the Flamma Bridge interface [here](https://app.flamma.network/bridge).

## Step 2: Withdraw Your Assets

1. Select the target network (Ethereum, Optimism, Arbitrum, Base).

2. Choose the asset you want to withdraw (ETH, USDT, USDC).

3. Click on the "Withdraw now" button to confirm asset transfer from the source network to Flamma Network.

![bridge](/images/deposit_1.png)

## 4. Confirm the Transaction

1. Your Web3 wallet will prompt you to confirm the transaction.

2. Review the transaction details and confirm.

## 5. Wait for Confirmation

1. The transaction will be processed on the blockchain.

2. Once confirmed, the assets will appear on the Flamma network.
