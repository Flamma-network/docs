# Bridge tokens

Flamma Network supports bridging assets (ETH, USDT, USDC) across Ethereum, Optimism (OP), Arbitrum (ARB), and Base. This tutorial will guide you through the process of depositing assets using the Flamma Bridge.



## Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

Visit the Flamma Bridge interface [here]() and connect your wallet (e.g., Metamask)

![bridge](/images/deposit_0.png)

## Step 2: Deposit

1. Choose the source network (Ethereum, Optimism, Arbitrum, Base).

2. Select the asset you want to bridge (ETH, USDT, USDC).

3. Click on the "Deposit now" button to confirm asset transfer from the source network to Flamma Network.

![bridge](/images/deposit_1.png)

## 4. Confirm the Transaction

1. Your Web3 wallet will prompt you to confirm the transaction.

2. Review the transaction details and confirm.

## 5. Wait for Confirmation

1. The transaction will be processed on the blockchain.

2. Once confirmed, the assets will appear on the Flamma network.
